KALE Woodwind Quartet

Refining the atmosphere of community through classical excellence.

The Vision

"To bring joy, comfort, and connection to seniors through live music — inspiring cherished memories and strengthening community through the power of performance."

The Mission

KALE Quartet is a student-led ensemble dedicated to sharing expressive, high-quality music with senior communities, brightening days and creating meaningful moments for audiences who deserve to feel celebrated.
