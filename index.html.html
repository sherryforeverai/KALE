KALE Quartet

Live music for senior communities.

Our Vision

To bring joy, comfort, and connection to seniors through live music — inspiring cherished memories and strengthening community.

Our Mission

We are a student-led ensemble dedicated to sharing high-quality woodwind performances with senior centers and community events, creating meaningful moments for audiences who deserve to be celebrated.
